ENCRYPTION AND PROTOCOL POLICY
Version: 2.1
Last Reviewed: February 2026
Purpose
Defines standards for data encryption and cryptographic protocols used across internal and external network
systems.
Policy Statements
1. TLS 1.3 or higher must be used for all internal communications.
2. Sensitive data must be encrypted in transit and at rest using AES-256.
3. Encryption keys must be rotated every 12 months.
4. MD5 and SHA-1 hashing algorithms are strictly prohibited for digital signatures.
