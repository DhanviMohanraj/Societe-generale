PASSWORD SECURITY POLICY
Version: 1.0
Last Reviewed: January 2025
Purpose
This policy defines password management requirements for all employees.
Policy Statements
1. Employees must rotate passwords every 90 days.
2. Each employee is required to rotate passwords every 90 days.
3. Passwords must never be shared with anyone.
4. No employee shall share passwords with anyone.
5. Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is mandatory for all user accounts.
6. Every user account shall require multi-factor authentication.
